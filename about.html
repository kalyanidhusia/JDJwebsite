About

JDJ Insight Partners

A consultancy focused on rigorous economic-policy analysis and clear, practical guidance for tax policy practitioners and state and local organizations.

Who I Am

I’m Joseph Johns, founder of JDJ Insight Partners — a consultancy built on rigorous economic-policy analysis and a commitment to clarity, transparency, and actionable insight for tax policy practitioners and state and local policy organizations.

JDJ Insight Partners was created to provide innovative, responsible, and sustainable solutions to state and local tax and fiscal policy challenges. The work is grounded in careful empirical methods and a realistic view of institutional, legal, and political constraints.

What JDJ Insight Partners Brings

JDJ Insight Partners draws on experience that spans academia, policy workshops, and national non-profits. The goal is to bring the same level of service and care to clients that scholars bring to peer-reviewed research.

The firm’s portfolio includes multiple publications related to state and local tax policy, as well as media appearances and op-eds on topics such as individual income tax reform efforts across multiple states and more specialized tax policy questions.

Background and Perspective

My work has roots in my time as a policy-oriented MA Fellow at the Mercatus Center at George Mason University and as a Policy Analyst at the Tax Foundation. Those roles gave me a deep understanding of economic frameworks, state and local tax and fiscal policy, and their real-world implications.

My academic and professional experience has centered on translating complex data and policy nuance into practical guidance for clients, stakeholders, and decision-makers. I have helped policymakers and external contributors to tax reform efforts craft robust, reliable, and sustainable policy solutions that aim to serve both taxpayers and client interests.

How I Work

I founded JDJ Insight Partners to bridge the gap between raw numbers and policy decisions. I use analytical rigor and a data-driven mindset to help organizations understand how shifting tax, regulatory, or economic conditions can affect their operations and long-term goals — and to chart a course that balances risk, compliance, and opportunity.

JDJ Insight Partners is built on digging into the details — tax codes, economic indicators, empirical models, and interactive data visualization tools — and surfacing the insights that matter. The focus is not just on scholarship, but on what works for practitioners and policymakers who need clear and timely guidance.

I value clarity, transparency, and communication. Even dense policy analysis should be accessible to the people who rely on it to make decisions.

Let’s Connect

If you are looking for clear-headed, realistic, and actionable analysis of the policy and tax environment — especially at the state and local level — I’d be glad to talk.
